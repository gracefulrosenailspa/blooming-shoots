About Us

Welcome to Blooming Shoots Education Center. We are a Christian-based institution dedicated to providing a nurturing environment for children from Playgroup to Grade 4.

Our curriculum is fully aligned with the CBE (Competency-Based Education) framework, ensuring our learners develop through a hands-on, practical approach.

School Programs & Services

Academics

Playgroup, PP1, PP2, and Grade 1 to 4 following the modern CBE curriculum.

Daycare

Safe and stimulating daycare services for our youngest learners with expert caregivers.

Transport

Reliable and safe school transport services covering Kasarani, Mwiki, and surrounding areas.

Extracurricular

Music, Chess, Swimming, French, Coding, and a dedicated Feeding Program.
